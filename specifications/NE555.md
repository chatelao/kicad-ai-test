# NE555 Precision Timer

## Description
The NE555 is a precision timing circuit capable of producing accurate time delays or oscillation. In the time-delay or monostable mode of operation, the timed interval is controlled by a single external resistor and capacitor network. In the astable mode of operation, the frequency and duty cycle are controlled independently with two external resistors and a single external capacitor.

## Pin Configuration (PDIP-8)
1. GND: Ground
2. TRIG: Trigger (starts timing)
3. OUT: Output
4. RESET: Reset (active low)
5. CONT: Control Voltage
6. THRES: Threshold (ends timing)
7. DISCH: Discharge
8. VCC: Supply Voltage (4.5V to 16V)

## Astable Operation Equations
- High time ($T_H$): $0.693 \times (R1 + R2) \times C1$
- Low time ($T_L$): $0.693 \times R2 \times C1$
- Period ($T$): $T_H + T_L = 0.693 \times (R1 + 2 \times R2) \times C1$
- Frequency ($f$): $1 / T$
