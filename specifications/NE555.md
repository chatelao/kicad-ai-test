# NE555 Precision Timer

## Pinout
1. GND
2. TRIG
3. OUT
4. RESET
5. CONT
6. THRES
7. DISCH
8. VCC

## Typical Astable Circuit (Blinking LED)
- VCC to Pin 8 and 4.
- GND to Pin 1.
- Resistor R1 between VCC and Pin 7.
- Resistor R2 between Pin 7 and Pin 6.
- Pin 6 and 2 shorted.
- Capacitor C1 between Pin 2 and GND.
- LED and Resistor R3 in series between Pin 3 and GND (or Pin 3 and VCC).
- Pin 5 decoupled with 10nF cap to GND (optional but recommended).
